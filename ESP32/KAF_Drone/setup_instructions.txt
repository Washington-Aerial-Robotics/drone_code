ESP32 Setup Instructions:
1) Download and extract the KAF_Drone folder from the GitHub repository.
2) Move the KAF_Drone folder to the location "C:\Users\[USERNAME]\Documents\Arduino\libraries\".
3) Open "C:\Users\[USERNAME]\Documents\Arduino\libraries\KAF_Drone\examples\setup\setup.ino" in ArduinoIDE.
4) Open libraries, then download the library for "DW3000".
5) Connect your ESP32 chip to your computer, then flash the Arduino sketch onto the chip.
